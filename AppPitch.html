App Pitch

In one of my courses, we were tasked with designing an app to enhance the student experience. I conceptualized Cornhusker Connections, an application aimed at helping University of Nebraska students cultivate stronger connections on campus, both socially and academically. I crafted a comprehensive business plan detailing how the app would promote a sense of community by linking students to study groups, campus events, and networking opportunities. Additionally, the app would feature tutoring services, mentorship programs, and a real-time chat function to facilitate seamless communication among peers.
