App Pitch

In one of my classes, we were asked to create an app that would benefit students. I came up with the idea for Cornhusker Connections, an app designed to help University of Nebraska students build stronger connections on campus, both socially and academically. I developed a business plan that outlined how the app would foster a sense of community by connecting students with study groups, campus events, and networking opportunities. The app would also include features like tutoring services, mentorship programs, and a real-time chat function for easy communication between peers.
