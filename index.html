My Portfolio

My name is Alyssa Perrotto, and I’m from Omaha, Nebraska. I attended the University of Nebraska-Lincoln from 2019 to 2023, where I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Journalism with a major in Advertising and Public Relations and a minor in Digital Humanities. This portfolio highlights both my college work and my professional experiences from the past year. After graduating, I relocated to Sterling, Colorado—a small town with limited opportunities in my field. Despite this, I found ways to apply my skills. I worked as a server at a local restaurant called Hootenannies, where I assisted the owner with menu redesigns and marketing materials, which you can view under that section of my portfolio. I also worked with a crystal shop, helping to manage their social media presence. Even in a small town, I was able to use my education and build on my experience. I’m now preparing to move to Kansas City in search of new opportunities that will allow me to continue learning and advancing my career. I appreciate you taking the time to explore my work, and I look forward to any feedback you might have.
