Sporting Kc Campaign

For my last semester of college my class partnered with Sporting Kc. We worked as teams to create different types of campaigns for them. My team worked together to create a pickup game on campus. We teamed up with the University's Womens Soccer Club and we were sponsored by Sporting Kc. I helped create T-shirts and gather equipment for our event. I created flyers, ads, and Instagram posts leading up to the event.
